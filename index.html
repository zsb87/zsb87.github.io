Research


My research area is human activity recognition and machine learning, using physical and physiological sensing data. With rich academic project experiences and health industrial experiences, I have grown a passion for applying machine learning and deep learning techniques to help advance our ability to perceive and understand human behaviors, facilitate our life in multiple dimensions, and engage in the development of the top-class products of high impact.
